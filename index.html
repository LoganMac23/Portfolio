About Me

I am from Southern York County, Pennsylvania, and I am currently studying interactive and graphic design and art at Millersville University. I have always enjoyed creating art, and I was introduced to digital art and design in my senior year of high school. Now a senior at Millersville, I still enjoy working with both physical and digital media. Though I have gained experience in multiple fields of design, I am most interested in working on branding, including designing logos and packaging designs, as well as developing 3D designs and products.
